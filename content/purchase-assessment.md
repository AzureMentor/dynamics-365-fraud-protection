# Purchase assessment

Microsoft Dynamics 365 Fraud Protection provides merchants the capability to assess e-commerce purchase transactions for fraudulent activity, such as stolen payment instruments or illegitimate purchases. By using Dynamics 365 Fraud Protection to evaluate the current state of your systems, and then utilizing powerful tools like the customizable rules engine and the risk support toolset, you can fine-tune your fraud protection decisions and implement new strategies based on the knowledge shared through Microsoftâ€™s fraud protection network.
